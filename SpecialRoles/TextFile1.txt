Role list

Jester (neutral) (must get voted off the ship to win)

Vigilante (good) (can shoot and kill a player once a game during a meeting by using a chat command)

Arsonist (evil) (can periodically douse players in gasoline by touching them, then light all doused players on fire, killing them, during a meeting using a chat command)

Gunsmith (good) (can give a player a gun once a game, which that player can use to shoot anyone once with during a meeting using a chat command)

Old Maid (neutral) (touch a player to swap roles with them. Old Maid cannot win)
